How to Play

Quick Start

  • Each level gives you a fixed number of turns.
  • On every turn, click exactly one tile before the time bar empties.
  • Your points usually come from:
    Tile Value √ó Your Multiplier.
  • Your Level Score must meet the Level Goal to advance.
  • Missing a turn gives 0 points and breaks your chain.

The Game Loop

  • Each level begins with a fresh board of tiles.
  • Pick one tile per turn before the timer runs out.
  • The chosen tile becomes used, but the rest of the board stays the same.
  • Repeat this process until all turns are used.
  • If your Level Score meets the goal, you advance to the next level.
  • If you miss the goal, your run ends (unless you spend a retry credit).

Scoring

  • Base Score: Tile Value √ó Multiplier
  • Chain Scoring:
    Consecutive green tiles increase the Chain Multiplier. Breaking the chain resets it.
  • Missed Turn:
    0 points, chain broken, time bonus reduced.
  • Speed Bonus:
    Finishing turns quickly banks time. Leftover time becomes bonus points at the end of the level (up to a capped percentage).

Strategy Tips

  • Early turns: grab Bonus tiles to build multiplier.
  • Mid-level: look for Chain opportunities for big gains.
  • If behind the goal: consider Risk or Rarity tiles.
  • If ahead: play safe and avoid negative swings.
  • Watch Goal %, Remaining Points, and Chain to adjust your strategy.

Common Mistakes

  • Staring at the board too long ‚Üí missed turns.
  • Ignoring Bonus tiles early.
  • Breaking chains by accident.
  • Taking every Risk tile without context.
  • Thinking total score matters per level (it doesn‚Äôt).
  • Underestimating the time bar in later levels.

Advanced Details

  • Tile distribution shifts at higher levels ‚Äî more risk, more rarity, stronger bonuses.
  • Chain multipliers scale slightly with level, rewarding consistent play.
  • Speed bonus is capped to prevent runaway scoring but remains strategically important.
