Quick Start

  • On every turn, you must click exactly one tile before the time bar empties.

Levels & Goals

  • Each level has a score target for that level only.
  • Your total Level Score is the sum of all turns in that level.
  • If your Level Score meets or beats the target, you advance to the next level.
  • As levels go up, you get:
    • Less time per move, so you must think faster.
    • More turns per level, with tougher targets.
    • Spicier tile distributions (more risk, more rarity, stronger chains).
  • Missing turns no longer ends your run, but wasted turns make targets harder.
